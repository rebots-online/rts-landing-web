Before you ask the score

A few grounded answers.

Is this just a camera pointed at an AI chatbot?+

No. A traceable pipeline separates image geometry, OMR, canonical score events, corrections, analysis, and prose. The language layer receives bounded facts and cannot become canonical score truth.

Does it require the cloud?+

Previously processed projects, corrections, deterministic analysis, notes, playback, and static overlays are designed to work locally. Recognition can run through an optional desktop sidecar, a LAN node, a remote provider, or a future on-device adapter.

Is the AI music lab part of score analysis?+

No. It is an optional, clearly labelled generative demonstration hosted by a Hugging Face Space. The core score graph, corrections, evidence-linked analysis, playback, and offline explanations do not depend on it.

Will it read dense Chopin perfectly?+

No credible first release should promise that. Release claims begin with clean printed keyboard repertoire and are stratified by difficulty. Dense cross-staff Romantic notation is accepted but not promised at the first gate.

Why not simply use MusicXML as the internal model?+

MusicXML is the interchange boundary. Selection, evidence, revision, timing, analysis, and stable page anchors need a canonical score graph with durable identity and explicit relations.
